Welcome to the Cadet Connection Portal. This digital platform serves as the official hub for all NCC cadets, officers, and staff of the North East Region. Together, we uphold the values of Unity and Discipline that NCC stands for. I encourage all cadets to make full use of this portal for their training, certifications, and growth.

Jai Hind!

NCC Motto & Pledge

"Unity and Discipline"

"Ekta aur Anushasan"

NCC Pledge

We, the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We will abide by the ideals of the NCC — selfless service and discipline. We shall always be prepared to serve our nation with dedication, and strive to be worthy citizens of India.

Jai Hind!
