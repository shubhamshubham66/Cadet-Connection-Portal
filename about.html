About NCC in North East India

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) in North East India has been instrumental in developing leadership, character, and discipline among the youth of the region since its establishment. The NER Directorate oversees more than 10 battalions across all 8 north-eastern states.

Vision: To empower volunteers by providing leadership development through military training, to produce an organized, trained, and motivated youth resource for national development.

Mission: To develop leadership and character qualities, ideals of selfless service, and foster the spirit of togetherness among the youth.
